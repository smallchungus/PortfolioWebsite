# ADR-005: Project Showcase - Quality Over Quantity

## Status
Accepted

## Context
Portfolio needs to demonstrate technical skills effectively. Options: show many projects (breadth) vs few projects (depth). FAANG hiring emphasizes impact and depth over quantity of side projects.

## Decision
Quality over quantity approach with 3-4 carefully curated projects:
- **Maximum 4 projects**: Deep dives with metrics and impact
- **Live demos required**: No "coming soon" or broken links
- **Metrics-driven**: User numbers, performance gains, business impact
- **Tech stack transparency**: Visible badges and implementation details
- **GitHub integration**: Stars, language stats, commit activity
- **Bento grid layout**: Asymmetric visual hierarchy

## Consequences
**Positive:**
- Demonstrates ability to ship and maintain production code
- Shows business impact awareness (metrics, user focus)
- Quality projects stand out more than quantity
- Easier to maintain and keep updated
- Better storytelling with detailed case studies

**Negative:**
- Fewer examples of technology breadth
- Pressure to maintain live demos indefinitely
- May not showcase experimental or learning projects

## Project Selection Criteria
- **Impact**: Measurable business or user outcome
- **Technical complexity**: Demonstrates relevant skills
- **Production quality**: Professional code, documentation, testing
- **Live demo**: Functional, deployed, accessible
- **Unique value**: Something that differentiates from generic projects
